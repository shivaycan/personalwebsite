Shivay and Dumbways

A minimalist blog for ornate ideas, built with calm, heat, and structure.

Biography

Hi, I’m Shivay.

I write about craft, technology, and the quiet disciplines that shape good work. Dumbways is my studio: part workshop, part archive, part chapel. I’m drawn to the weight of stone, the patience of sculpture, and the precision of form.

This site is an experiment in minimalism that still feels ceremonial — a place for essays, notes, and the kind of attention that keeps work honest.

“If you keep the edges clean, the center can breathe.” — personal rule
