I build in public for one reason:
it turns vague ambition into accountable progress.

Publishing notes forces decisions.
It exposes weak assumptions early.
It creates a timeline of how ideas evolved,
not just polished end results.

Over time, this becomes a personal operating system.
I can revisit tradeoffs, reuse patterns, and move with less friction.

Public work is not about showing off.
It is about thinking clearly where others can challenge it.
