Minimalism does not have to be cold.

I am interested in a version of minimal design that keeps discipline,
but still carries warmth, rhythm, and cultural memory.
In an Indian context, that can mean ceremonial spacing,
expressive typography, and restrained ornament instead of visual clutter.

The goal is not to recreate tradition literally.
The goal is to translate its feeling into digital form.

When done well, interfaces can feel modern and rooted at the same time.
