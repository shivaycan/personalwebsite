Indian visual culture is expressive, layered, and rhythmic.

In web design, I reinterpret that through contrast, ornament balance,
and ceremonial spacing rather than noisy decoration.

The intent is not imitation.
It is translation: bringing warmth, depth, and identity
into modern digital systems.
