date: "2026-03-08"
tags:

---

I build in public because it keeps my work accountable.

When I write down decisions, constraints, and tradeoffs,
I stop optimizing for perfection and start optimizing for clarity.

Over time, notes become infrastructure.
They help me revisit old ideas, avoid repeated mistakes,
and move faster with better judgment.
